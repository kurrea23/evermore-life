Same voice settings. Generate as one continuous file.

**Save as:** `s2_voiceover_full.mp3`

```

A small kitchen.
A tired parent.
A child who wanted to help.

But love has a way of becoming direction.

Years later, she opened her own restaurant.
And the first meal she served was for the person who taught her.

The little things you do become the roots they grow from.
```

Target runtime: 30–38 seconds at 0.85x speed.
