## STEP 2 — Voiceover in ElevenLabs

Same settings (warm male, 0.85x speed).
**Save as:** `s3_voiceover_full.mp3`

**Paste this script:**
```
They may not remember every late night.
But they will remember that you never gave up.

Every shift.
Every sacrifice.
Every quiet moment when you chose their future over your comfort.

One day, they walk across the stage.
And suddenly, every hard day has a face.

That is legacy.
```

Target runtime: 30–42 seconds at 0.85x speed.
