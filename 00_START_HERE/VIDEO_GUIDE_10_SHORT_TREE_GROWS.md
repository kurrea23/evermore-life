## STEP 3 — Music

Search Artlist: `minimal ambient piano single note peaceful`
Filter: Very minimal, under 30 seconds, almost silent — just texture.
BPM: 50 or slower.

**Optional sound design (CapCut):**

## STEP 7 — Color Grade

Most cinematic of all the guides:
Brightness +2 / Contrast +12 / Saturation -18 / Temperature +6 / Vignette 25% / Sharpen +8

This video should feel like it was shot on film in 1972. Warm, soft, almost dreamlike.
