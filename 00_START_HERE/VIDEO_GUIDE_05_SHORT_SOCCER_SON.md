## STEP 2 — Voiceover in ElevenLabs

Same voice settings as Guide 04 (warm male, 0.85x speed, Stability 55, Clarity 75).

Generate as ONE continuous audio file.

**Save as:** `s1_voiceover_full.mp3`

**Paste this entire script:**
```
He once had a dream of his own.
Then life gave him something bigger to protect.

Years later, his son picked up the same dream —
not because he was pushed,
but because something inside him already knew.

So his father showed up.
Practice after practice.
Shift after shift.
Year after year.

When the world finally cheered for his son,
the first person he ran to was his dad.

Some dreams do not end.
They move through the people we love.
```

Total runtime should be approximately 35–42 seconds at 0.85x speed.
