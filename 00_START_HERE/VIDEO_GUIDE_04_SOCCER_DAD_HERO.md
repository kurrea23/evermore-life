## STEP 5 — Music Mix in CapCut

1. Place music track on the audio layer below all voiceover tracks
2. Set music volume: **18%** (just texture, never competes with VO)
3. VO volume: **100%**
4. Fade music IN over first 3 seconds
5. Fade music OUT over last 5 seconds
6. At 4:35 (CTA section): music volume DOWN to 10%

## STEP 8 — Color Grade in CapCut

Apply to the ENTIRE timeline (select all clips → Adjust):

- Brightness: +5
- Contrast: +10
- Saturation: -10 (slightly desaturate for cinematic look)
- Temperature: +8 (warmer)
- Vignette: 15% (subtle dark edges)
- Sharpen: +5

This gives you the warm, slightly desaturated film look from the visual style bible.
