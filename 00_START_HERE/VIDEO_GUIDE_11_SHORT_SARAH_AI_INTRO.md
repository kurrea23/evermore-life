# VIDEO GUIDE 11 — Short Form: Sarah AI Assistant Intro
## 30 second direct response / trust builder
**Bridge video. Connects emotional storytelling to the practical next step.**

---

## WHAT YOU'RE BUILDING

30 second vertical video (9:16). Sarah introduces herself and explains the coverage review.
Runs in Campaign B (MOF) to warm viewers ready for the next step.
Uses HeyGen (Sarah avatar) OR ElevenLabs voiceover over clean motion graphics.

**Two production options — pick one:**
- **Option A:** HeyGen AI avatar as Sarah (most powerful — puts a face to the brand)
- **Option B:** Clean graphic/animation with voiceover (faster to produce)

**Time estimate: 1–2 hours**

---

## OPTION A — HeyGen Avatar as Sarah

### STEP A1 — HeyGen Setup

Go to: `heygen.com` → Avatar Library

**Creating Sarah:**
1. Pick a female AI avatar that looks warm, professional, early 30s, approachable
2. Recommended: search "Sara" or "Jennifer" in HeyGen avatar library
3. Voice: choose a warm female voice or use ElevenLabs voice synced in HeyGen
4. Background: clean white or light gradient (you'll overlay brand elements in CapCut)

### STEP A2 — Paste this script into HeyGen

```
Hi, I'm Sarah with Evermore Life.

I can help you start with a few simple questions so a licensed broker can review what coverage may fit your situation.

We may ask about your age, state, health, budget, and what you want to protect.

No pressure. No confusion. Just a clearer next step.

Click below to start your coverage review.
```

### STEP A3 — HeyGen Settings

- Aspect ratio: 9:16
- Resolution: 1080x1920
- File name after export: `s7_heygen_sarah_raw.mp4`

### STEP A4 — Bring Into CapCut

1. Import `s7_heygen_sarah_raw.mp4`
2. Add a branded lower third (bottom of screen):
   - Text: `Sarah | Evermore Life`
   - Font: clean sans-serif, white, dark background bar
   - Duration: full video
3. Add end card (last 5 seconds):
   ```
   Start Your Coverage Review →
   evermorelife.org
   ```
4. Auto-generate captions → review → correct
5. Disclaimer text (very small, bottom):
   ```
   Coverage options vary by age, health, state, carrier, and eligibility. Not all applicants qualify.
   ```
6. Export as: `ELC_Short_SarahIntro_9x16_v01.mp4`

### STEP B2 — Motion Graphic Build in CapCut

New project → 1080x1920 (9:16). 30fps.
Background: White or very light cream (#FAFAF5).

**Text reveal timeline:**

| Time | Text | Animation |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:03 | `Hi, I'm Sarah.` | Fade in, large serif |
| 0:03–0:07 | `with Evermore Life` | Fade in below first line |
| 0:08–0:13 | `I help families compare life insurance options.` | Slide up |
| 0:14–0:19 | `Age · State · Health · Budget · Goals` | Each word fades in 0.2 sec apart |
| 0:20–0:24 | `No pressure. No confusion.` | Bold, centered |
| 0:25–0:28 | `Just a clearer next step.` | Italic, smaller |
| 0:28–0:30 | `Start Your Coverage Review →` + `evermorelife.org` | CTA box, gold button style |

**Font:** Mix of serif (story text) and sans-serif (CTA) for contrast
**Colors:** Navy (#0A1628) text on cream, Gold (#C9A94D) CTA button

### STEP B3 — Add Sarah "Avatar" Illustration

3. Text builds in lower half

### STEP B4 — Export (Option B)

File name: `ELC_Short_SarahIntro_9x16_v01.mp4`
