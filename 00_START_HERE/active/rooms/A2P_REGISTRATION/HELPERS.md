# A2P ROOM — WHO DOES WHAT

Kevin (project manager) keeps you moving. The other helpers each have a lane.

| Helper | Good at in this room | NOT for |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Kevin V2** | Reminds you which phase you're on. Asks "did you take the screenshot yet?" Won't let you submit if a stop condition is unmet. | Doing the work for you. |
| **Coworker (Claude)** | Editing copy if a GHL field has a length cap. Drafting a response to a TCR rejection notice. Cross-checking that page text matches campaign text. Reading rejected messages and explaining what carriers flagged. | Submitting the form. Touching your GHL account. |
| **Codex** | Code-side fixes if the page needs to be re-published with corrected consent text. Re-running the publish/deploy scripts in `00_START_HERE/active/run/`. | Anything in the GHL UI. |
| **Claude (general)** | Explaining A2P concepts you don't recognize ("what's a brand vs. a campaign?"). Looking up TCR rejection codes. | Speculating about whether you'll get approved — only TCR knows. |

## Decision rule

> If a step takes a human < 10 minutes AND is part of learning the system, the human does it.
> AI is allowed to set up scaffolding, draft text, and check for mistakes.
> AI is never allowed to click "Submit" in GHL, take screenshots, or send the verification SMS.
