# VIDEO GUIDE 09 — Short Form: Broker Education Clip
## 45–60 second direct response / trust video
**MOF education content. Runs to warm viewers. Explains what you do and why.**

---

## WHAT YOU'RE BUILDING

45–60 second vertical video (9:16). Direct, clear, no emotional story — pure education.
Runs as Campaign B (MOF) education creative to people who watched 25%+ of TOF videos.
This one does NOT use AI footage. It uses HeyGen (talking head) OR screen graphics.

**Two production options — pick one:**
- **Option A:** HeyGen talking head video (you or an AI avatar explains it)
- **Option B:** Animated text / graphic video (no talking head — just text and icons in CapCut)

**Time estimate: 1–2 hours**

---

## OPTION A — HeyGen Talking Head

Use this if you want a spokesperson on screen.

### STEP A1 — HeyGen Setup

Go to: `heygen.com` → Instant Avatar OR choose an AI avatar

**If using yourself (most authentic):**
1. HeyGen → Instant Avatar → record 2 minutes of yourself talking naturally (follow HeyGen's consent script)
2. Wait for avatar processing (15–30 min)
3. Use your avatar for this video

**If using HeyGen AI avatar:**
1. HeyGen → Avatar Library → pick a professional male or female avatar
2. Choose one that looks warm and trustworthy, not corporate
3. Recommended: "Matthew" or "Aria" voice match

### STEP A2 — HeyGen Script (paste into HeyGen text-to-video)

```
Life insurance is not one-size-fits-all.

A 35-year-old parent protecting a mortgage may need a very different type of coverage than a 68-year-old planning for final expenses.

That is why at Evermore Life, we start by asking about your age, health, budget, state, and goals first.

Then we compare available options from carriers you may actually qualify for.

Some people need term life — affordable, straightforward income protection.

Some need final expense coverage — smaller amounts, easier to qualify for.

And some are looking at indexed universal life — permanent coverage that also builds cash value for retirement.

Our job is to match the right coverage to the right person. Not to push the most expensive product.

Takes about 15 minutes. No pressure.

Start with a simple coverage review at evermorelife.org.
```

### STEP A3 — HeyGen Export Settings

- Resolution: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Quality: High
- File name: `s5_heygen_raw.mp4`

### STEP A4 — Bring into CapCut

1. Import `s5_heygen_raw.mp4`
2. Add captions: CapCut → Captions → Auto-generate → review and correct
3. Add end card (last 5 seconds):
   ```
   Evermore Life
   evermorelife.org
   Start Your Coverage Review →
   ```
4. Color grade: Brightness +3 / Contrast +8 (lighter grade — this is a direct-camera shot, not cinematic)
5. Export as: `ELC_Short_BrokerEd_9x16_v01.mp4`
