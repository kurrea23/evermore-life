# Retirement Planning Website Launch Handoff

## What Exists

The live Evermore Life V2 website was copied into this separate folder and repositioned for a broader, younger, long-term planning audience. Existing website files, current GHL pages, and current deployment files were left untouched.

The primary conversion is now a free strategy review rather than a final-expense quote.

## Positioning

Primary promise:

> Build for retirement. Protect the people you love.

Primary audience:

- Working families and homeowners
- Adults actively building retirement savings
- Parents who need income protection
- People exploring IUL or annuity strategies
- Business owners and professionals with legacy or succession concerns

Primary products:

- IUL and permanent life strategies
- Term and family income protection
- Mortgage protection
- Annuities and retirement income
- Legacy and estate liquidity

## Launch Sequence

1. Form or confirm the legal entity and licensed business name.
2. Confirm the domain and business contact information.
3. Create a separate GHL sub-account or clearly separated pipeline and workflow lane.
4. Build a new intake form with fields for goal, age range, state, timeline, existing coverage, mortgage, and preferred contact time.
5. Build tags and pipeline stages prefixed with `retirement-` or another unique lane identifier.
6. Connect a separate calendar and follow-up workflow.
7. Review IUL, annuity, tax, and retirement language with carrier/compliance resources.
8. Replace the copied GHL form ID and test one complete lead.
9. Connect a separate GHL chat widget. The copied live-site widget is intentionally disabled.
10. Update canonical URLs, Worker routes, sitemap, and robots.
11. Remove `noindex, nofollow`, deploy, and run desktop/mobile/link/form checks.

## Required GHL Separation

## Compliance Guardrails

- Do not describe IUL as an investment.
- Do not promise tax-free income, guaranteed market gains, or a universal zero-loss outcome.
- Explain that policy performance depends on product design, funding, charges, caps, participation rates, underwriting, and carrier terms.
- Label illustrations and examples as educational and non-guaranteed.
- Do not present insurance agents as financial, tax, or legal advisers unless properly licensed and authorized.
- Confirm referral and co-marketing arrangements before paying or receiving compensation.
