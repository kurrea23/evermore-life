Frequently Asked Questions

Questions Before the Call

A few direct answers about licensing, support, fit, and expectations.

Do I need to already be licensed?

Licensed agents are welcome to apply. If you are not licensed yet, we can discuss the licensing path and whether this opportunity may be a fit.

Are results or income guaranteed?

No. Results depend on licensing, effort, skill, consistency, market conditions, lead flow, compliance, and follow-through. Evermore Life Insurance LLC does not guarantee income or specific results.

What kind of agent is Evermore Life Insurance LLC looking for?

We are looking for agents who care about protecting families, are coachable, can follow a system, communicate well, produce with integrity, and want to grow as leaders.

Will agents receive training?

The organization is being built around training, sales process support, CRM workflows, AI-assisted tools, repeatable daily activity, and leadership development.

Is this only for final expense agents?

No. Evermore Life Insurance LLC is building around family protection, life insurance, mortgage protection, IUL education where appropriate, and other approved life insurance solutions depending on licensing, state availability, carrier rules, and client suitability.

How do I start?

Request a 30-minute team-fit call so we can discuss your goals, licensing status, market, and whether Evermore Life Insurance LLC is aligned with what you want to build.
