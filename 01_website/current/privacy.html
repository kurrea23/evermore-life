Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2, 2026  |  Last Updated: May 2, 2026

Summary: Evermore Life collects your information to provide insurance quotes and communicate with you. We do not sell your personal information. SMS consent is optional, separate from quote requests, and you can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP.

1. Who We Are

Evermore Life ("we," "our," or "us") is an independent insurance brokerage operating at evermorelife.org. We help individuals and families review life insurance, final expense, term life, indexed universal life (IUL), whole life, mortgage protection, and annuity options. Our contact email is evermorelifeagent01@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and mailing address.
  • Health & Insurance Information: Date of birth, health history, coverage needs, and beneficiary details — only when needed to generate an accurate insurance quote.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our website (collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools).
  • Communication Records: Records of calls, texts, emails, and form submissions with our agents.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, process, and fulfill insurance quote requests.
  • To communicate with you by phone, email, or SMS text message regarding your quote, policy options, and account.
  • To send you informational and promotional communications about our products and services (you may opt out at any time).
  • To comply with applicable legal, regulatory, and licensing requirements.
  • To improve our website, services, and marketing efforts.
  • To detect fraud and protect the security of our systems.

4. SMS / Text Message Communications

If you voluntarily check an SMS consent box on one of our forms, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages from Evermore Life, including:

  • Responses to your insurance quote requests
  • Follow-up messages from our licensed agents
  • Informational messages about coverage options and policy updates

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier plan.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any text message at any time. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and will receive no further texts from us.

For help: Reply HELP to any message or contact us at evermorelifeagent01@gmail.com.

We do not share, sell, rent, or use your mobile phone number for any purpose other than the communications described above. Your opt-in to SMS is not a condition of purchasing any insurance product or service.

SMS consent data: All categories above exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent. SMS opt-in data and consent will not be shared with third parties or marketers for their own marketing or promotional purposes.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Insurance Carriers: To obtain quotes and process applications on your behalf, with your authorization.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist us with technology, CRM, marketing, and communications (e.g., GoHighLevel CRM platform). These vendors are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with prior notice to affected users.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze traffic, improve user experience, and support our marketing efforts. You may control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

We may use third-party analytics tools such as Google Analytics. These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Typically, this means retaining contact and quote data for up to seven (7) years.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Delete your personal information (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time by replying STOP to SMS, clicking unsubscribe in emails, or contacting us directly.
  • Data portability — receive a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at evermorelifeagent01@gmail.com. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. Security
