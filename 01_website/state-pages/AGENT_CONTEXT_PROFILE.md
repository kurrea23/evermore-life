# Evermore State Expansion Agent Context

Use this profile as the shared context for every agent working on an Evermore
state page.

## Mission

Build a premium family of state-specific pages at `evermorelife.org/<state>/`
that feels unmistakably Evermore, earns trust locally, and routes visitors only
into service paths that are currently approved for their state.

The first wave is:

1. Arizona - active
2. Texas - active
3. Arkansas - active

The long-term system must scale to all 50 states without turning into 50
unmaintainable one-off pages.

## Truth Order

1. Verified live evidence and current license artifacts
2. Direct human operator correction or approval
3. Canonical repo source and current runbooks
4. State-page data and dated Blueprint reports
5. Older handoffs and chat history

When sources disagree, do not silently choose one. Mark the conflict and stop
any affected launch action.

## Non-Negotiable Compliance

- Never promise approval, price, savings, returns, or universal availability.
- Never say everyone qualifies.
- Never imply Evermore is an insurance carrier.
- Never invent state laws, statistics, endorsements, testimonials, offices, or
  carrier availability.
- Explain that coverage, pricing, and eligibility vary.
- Keep Privacy and Terms links visible.
- Keep every draft `noindex, nofollow` until launch approval.
- A pending state cannot enter the quote, booking, active nurture, or Sarah
  qualification flow.

Required footer disclosure:

> Coverage availability, policy type, benefits, premiums, and approval depend
> on age, health, state, carrier underwriting, product type, and other
> eligibility factors. Not all applicants qualify. Evermore Life Insurance LLC may work with
> multiple carriers to help compare available options. This is a solicitation
> for insurance.

## State Status Contract

Each state has exactly one service mode:

- `active`: a licensed/approved state that may use the coverage-review CTA.
- `pending`: a premium informational page that may collect only availability
  interest through a separately approved path.
- `unavailable`: no conversion CTA; provide general contact information only.

Changing a state mode requires:

1. Current license or service evidence
2. Human approval
3. Intake/workflow routing verification
4. Compliance review
5. A state data change and regenerated page

## Required State Research Output

Agents may personalize:

- the emotional opening;
- common life-stage priorities;
- broad geographic language;
- product education emphasis;
- FAQ ordering;
- local SEO phrasing that is factually supportable.

Agents must label as unknown until verified:

- state-specific insurance laws or deadlines;
- average costs, household statistics, and demographic claims;
- carrier/product availability;
- local office presence;
- state license numbers or producer details;
- reviews, testimonials, and performance claims.

## Definition Of Done

A state page is ready for human review when:

- its state record passes the generator validator;
- its mode and CTA agree;
- desktop and mobile layouts are visually reviewed;
- links, canonical, robots, and disclosures are correct;
- no unsupported claim is present;
- the Blueprint report records evidence and unknowns.

A state page is ready to publish only after the launch gates in
`STATE_PAGE_QA.md` are approved.
