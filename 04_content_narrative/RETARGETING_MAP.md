# Evermore Life — Retargeting Map
## Audience Buckets, Creative Strategy & Metrics

**Source:** Imported from Cinematic Legacy Campaign (08_Retargeting_Map.md) + expanded
**Platforms:** Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok, YouTube

---

## Core Principle

> Use emotional story content to build warm audiences. Retarget viewers with simple education and a clear appointment CTA. Never retarget with the same emotional content — graduate people up the funnel.

---

## The Four Audience Buckets

### Bucket 1 — Cold Audience (Top of Funnel)
People who have not engaged with Evermore Life yet.

**Creative:**
- Cinematic legacy story shorts (Soccer Dad clips, Chef Daughter, Graduation)
- Evermore tree/acorn motif brand spots
- "Your legacy moves on" awareness content

**Goal:** Video views, engagement, profile visits

**CTA (soft):**
- "Watch the story."
- "Be there evermore."

**Bid strategy:** Lowest cost / ThruPlay (15+ seconds)

---

### Bucket 2 — Engaged Viewers (Middle of Funnel)
People who watched 50–75%+ of a video or engaged with posts in the last 30 days.

**Creative:**
- "Here's what life insurance actually does."
- "How Evermore Life helps compare options."
- "What affects your monthly price?"
- "Term vs. final expense — which is right for you?"
- "What to expect on a coverage review."

**Goal:** Landing page visits, form starts, Sarah chat starts

**CTA:**
- "Start a simple coverage review."
- "See your options in minutes."

**Bid strategy:** Landing page views

---

### Bucket 3 — Page Visitors / Form Starts (Bottom of Funnel)
People who visited the funnel page or started but did not submit a form.

**Creative:**
- FAQ clips ("Do I need a medical exam?")
- Trust clips ("We work for you, not one company.")
- Process clips ("What happens on the call?")
- "No pressure, just clarity."

**Goal:** Completed form, appointment booking

**CTA:**
- "Finish your coverage review."
- "Pick a time that works for you."

**Bid strategy:** Conversions (Lead event)

---

### Bucket 4 — Leads Not Booked
People who submitted info but have not scheduled a call.

**Creative:**
- SMS/email sequences (Sarah follow-up — see GHL_SARAH_AI_FLOW.md)
- "What happens on the call?" video
- "3 things to expect from your coverage review"
- Warm, low-pressure reassurance

**Goal:** Scheduled appointment

**CTA:** "Pick a time to chat." / "Schedule my review."

---

### Bucket 5 — Booked / No-Show
People who booked but missed the appointment.

**Creative:**
- Gentle reschedule reminder (Sarah SMS — see GHL flow)
- "Life gets busy — we can restart."
- Evermore brand warmth: no guilt, just availability

**Goal:** Rescheduled appointment

**CTA:** "Reschedule my review."

---

## Retargeting Content Sequence (Day-by-Day)

### Days 0–1 (Immediately After Form Submission)
- Thank you page confirmation
- Sarah welcome SMS
- "What happens next" video (posted to confirmation page)

### Days 2–4
- Broker process explainer clip
- FAQ: "How much does it cost?" video
- FAQ: "What if I have health issues?" video

### Days 5–10
- Legacy story variation (different story than the one they originally watched)
- Educational content: "Coverage options explained"
- "Still have questions?" with live chat or Sarah CTA

### Days 11–30
- Monthly affordability framing ("Coverage starting under $X/mo for qualifying ages")
- Family protection education
- Final expense clarity video (for ICP2)
- Long-form nurture (see GHL_SARAH_AI_FLOW.md warm nurture message)

## Ad Naming Convention

Use this structure for all campaigns:

```
ELC_[Year]_[Quarter]_[Funnel Stage]_[ContentType]_[Story]_[Aspect]

Examples:
ELC_2026_Q2_TOF_LegacyStory_Soccer_9x16
ELC_2026_Q2_MOF_Education_HowItWorks_16x9
ELC_2026_Q2_BOF_CoverageReviewRetarget_9x16
ELC_2026_Q2_LEAD_FormStartFollowUp_SMS
```

**Funnel Stage codes:**
- TOF = Top of Funnel (cold)
- MOF = Middle of Funnel (warm/engaged)
- BOF = Bottom of Funnel (hot/form starter)
- LEAD = Post-lead nurture

## Key Metrics to Track

### Top of Funnel
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| 3-second video views | Track volume |
| ThruPlay (15+ sec) completion rate | >15% of impressions |
| Average watch time | >50% of video |
| Engagement rate (likes/shares/saves) | >2% |
| Cost per engaged viewer | <$0.10 |

### Middle of Funnel
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Landing page click-through rate | >2% |
| Cost per landing page view | <$1.50 |
| Form start rate (visitors who begin form) | >20% |
| Chat start rate | >10% |

### Bottom of Funnel
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Cost per lead (form submitted) | <$25 |
| Lead → appointment set rate | >30% |
| Appointment show rate | >60% |
| Show → application rate | >25% |
| Cost per booked call | <$75 |
| Cost per issued policy | Track and optimize |

## Audience Exclusions (Always Apply)

- Exclude current customers (email list match)
- Exclude people who already booked (tag in GHL → Meta/TikTok exclusion audience)
- Exclude people who replied STOP to SMS
- Exclude under-18 from all ads
