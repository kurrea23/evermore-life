### Audience Buckets in Meta Ads Manager (How to Build Them)

**Bucket 1 — Video Viewers (Warm)**
- Go to: Ads Manager → Audiences → Create → Custom Audience → Video
- Select: "People who watched at least 25% of your video"
- Select your TOF story videos
- Window: 60 days
- Name: `ELC_VideoViewers_25pct_60d`

**Bucket 2 — Engaged Viewers (Hot-ish)**
- Same as above, but: "People who watched at least 75%"
- Name: `ELC_VideoViewers_75pct_60d`

**Bucket 3 — Funnel Page Visitors**
- Custom Audience → Website → "People who visited specific pages"
- URL contains: your funnel page URL
- Window: 30 days
- Name: `ELC_FunnelVisitors_30d`

**Bucket 4 — Sarah Starters (Didn't Book)**
- Custom Audience → Website → "People who visited" Sarah intake URL
- Exclude: People who visited your thank-you / booking confirmation page
- Name: `ELC_SarahStarted_NoBook_30d`

**Lookalike Audiences (Run these once Bucket 1 hits 500+)**
- Lookalike of `ELC_VideoViewers_75pct_60d` → 1% US
