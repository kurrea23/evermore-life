## Review Checklist

Mark a take `Approved` only when all are true:

- People look natural and consistent.
- Hands, eyes, faces, and movement are usable.
- No accidental readable text or fake legal/insurance paperwork appears.
- The mood matches Evermore: warm, quiet, emotionally honest.
- The clip does not rely on fear, shock, or false urgency.
- Any claim language will be added in editing, not baked into questionable footage.
- The aspect ratio matches the intended platform.
