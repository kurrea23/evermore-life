## Platform Defaults

| Platform | Default Format | First Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| TikTok | 9:16, captions burned in, under 35 sec | TOF testing |
| Instagram Reels | 9:16, captions burned in, under 35 sec | TOF testing and retargeting |
| Facebook Feed | 1:1 or 4:5, captions burned in | Adult-child and warm retargeting |
| YouTube Shorts | 9:16, under 60 sec | Repurpose winners |
| YouTube / Facebook Native | 16:9 or 4:5, 60-180 sec | Hero and mid-funnel story assets |

## Decision Rule

After 7 days or 500+ impressions, keep the winner and pause the weak variant.

Evaluate in this order:

1. Watch retention.
2. Save/share behavior.
3. Landing page click-through.
4. Sarah start rate.
5. Booked appointment rate.

Do not scale an ad only because it is emotionally strong. Scale the ad that creates booked appointments.
