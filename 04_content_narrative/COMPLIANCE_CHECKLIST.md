# Evermore Life — Compliance Checklist
## Insurance Advertising & Platform Safety

**Source:** Imported from Cinematic Legacy Campaign (11_Compliance_Checklist.md)
**Note:** This is an operational checklist, not legal advice. Consult a licensed compliance professional before running paid advertising.

---

## Insurance Advertising Rules

- [ ] Do not promise approval unless the exact product allows it and all limitations are disclosed
- [ ] Do not say "everyone qualifies"
- [ ] Do not use exact premium examples without clear age/health/state/carrier context
- [ ] Do not imply a policy is free
- [ ] Do not hide that the offer is insurance
- [ ] Do not use fake testimonials
- [ ] Do not use fabricated client stories as if they are real (all Evermore Life stories should be presented as illustrative narratives, not real client testimonials)
- [ ] Do not imply Evermore Life is a carrier if it is an agency/brokerage
- [ ] Do not mention carriers unless contracted and allowed to advertise them
- [ ] Do not request sensitive financial or medical information directly inside ad copy
- [ ] Use proper SMS consent language before texting marketing messages
- [ ] Privacy Policy and Terms pages must be live and linked before running paid ads
- [ ] Keep all claims clear, truthful, and substantiated

---

## Platform Safety

### Meta (Facebook / Instagram)

- [ ] Use the correct **Special Ad Category** (Financial Products & Services) for insurance campaigns
- [ ] Target insurance ads only to adults (18+)
- [ ] Avoid direct requests for PII inside the ad itself
- [ ] Keep landing page consistent with ad content (no bait-and-switch)
- [ ] Avoid misleading "too good to be true" hooks
- [ ] Create video engagement custom audiences from compliant videos

### TikTok

- [ ] Follow financial services advertising requirements
- [ ] Be transparent that the offer relates to insurance
- [ ] Avoid misleading claims or pressure tactics
- [ ] Confirm whether approval/authorization is required for financial services ads in target markets

### YouTube

- [ ] Insurance ads may require Google Ads certification for financial services
- [ ] Ensure disclosures are visible in the video and in the ad description
- [ ] Avoid targeting minors

## Required Copy Blocks

### Funnel Page Footer Disclaimer
> Coverage availability, policy type, benefits, premiums, and approval depend on age, health, state, carrier underwriting, product type, and other eligibility factors. Not all applicants qualify. Evermore Life may work with multiple carriers to help compare available options. This is a solicitation for insurance.

### AI Chat (Sarah) Disclaimer
> I can help collect basic information and explain the process, but final recommendations, pricing, eligibility, and applications should be reviewed with a licensed insurance professional.

### Short-Form Caption Disclaimer
> Coverage options vary by age, health, state, product type, carrier, and eligibility. Not all applicants qualify.

### SMS Consent Copy (Required on all forms)
> By checking this box, I agree to receive calls, texts, and emails from Evermore Life regarding my insurance inquiry. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency may vary. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

## Safe Emotional Language Reference

**Use these:**
- protect
- prepare
- clarity
- peace of mind
- showing up
- legacy
- family protection
- coverage review
- options

**Avoid overusing:**
- "burden" (implies the parent is a burden — can read negatively)
- "death" (say "passing" or "if something happens to you")
- "leave them with nothing"
- "your family will suffer"
- "before it's too late"
- "guaranteed" (unless specifically a guaranteed-issue product with full disclosure)

---

## Story Content Framing

All cinematic story content (Soccer Dad, other legacy stories) should be:

- Presented as illustrative narratives, NOT as real client testimonials
- Labeled as "A story about legacy" or similar framing if required by state law
- Free of any specific policy details (prices, benefit amounts) unless reviewed by compliance
- Reviewed by a licensed compliance professional before paid distribution at scale

## Pre-Launch Compliance Checklist

Before going live with paid campaigns:
- [ ] Licensed in all states being targeted
- [ ] Funnel page reviewed by licensed compliance professional
- [ ] SMS opt-in language approved
- [ ] Carrier co-branding permissions confirmed (if using carrier logos)
- [ ] E&O (Errors & Omissions) coverage current
- [ ] DNC (Do Not Call) registry check process in place
- [ ] Lead data storage compliant with CCPA/applicable state laws
