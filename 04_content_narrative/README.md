# Evermore Life — Content Narrative System
## Folder: `04_content_narrative/`

---

## What This Is

This folder contains the complete content strategy and execution system for Evermore Life's brand narrative — from emotional concept through production-ready scripts, funnel infrastructure, and week-by-week launch plan.

Everything here is designed around a single conviction: **Evermore Life doesn't sell life insurance. It sells love.**

The content system is built to move two types of people — the protective parent and the adult child — from cold awareness to covered, using cinematic storytelling, a smart AI-powered funnel, and a compounding content engine that gets more effective the longer it runs.

## How the Pieces Connect

### The Emotional Core (Start Here)
Everything originates in the **NARRATIVE_BIBLE**. Before touching any content, anyone working on Evermore Life — copywriters, video producers, designers, ad buyers — should read it. It defines what the brand is, what it isn't, who it's speaking to, and how it sounds.

### The Story Engine
The **STORY_SCRIPTS** folder contains the actual creative content that powers the funnel:

- **Soccer Dad** is the Hero Video — the emotional anchor. It's long-form (5+ minutes), designed to convert warm or retargeted viewers who've already seen a short clip. This is the story that makes people feel something.
- **Tree/Acorn Brand Story** is the identity spot — shorter, more visual, used as a brand awareness unit and as an intro/outro to other content.
- **Short Form Hooks** are the top-of-funnel — 10 hooks designed to stop the scroll on TikTok/Reels/Shorts. Each has a 30-second arc that creates curiosity and drives viewers to the longer content.

### The Funnel Flow

```
SHORT FORM AD (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
        ↓
  [75%+ watch time triggers retarget pixel]
        ↓
  HERO VIDEO (Soccer Dad — YouTube/Facebook)
        ↓
  FUNNEL PAGE (FUNNEL_PAGE.html)
        ↓
  "Talk to Sarah" — AI Intake
        ↓
  Booked Appointment → Coverage
```

### The AI Video Production Layer
**AI_VIDEO_PROMPTS.md** translates the scripts into ready-to-paste generation prompts for Sora, Runway ML, and Midjourney. This enables rapid, low-cost production of cinematic content without traditional film crews.

### The Ad Campaign Scaffold
**ad_campaign_scaffold/** turns the narrative system into a repeatable ad factory. It contains the first production batch, campaign naming, Higgsfield MCP prompt brief, generated-output lanes, and a review tracker for deciding which videos are approved, exported, launched, paused, or scaled.

Start daily execution in
`ad_campaign_scaffold/CONTENT_ACTIVATION_BOARD.md`. It inventories real local
assets, points to paste-ready post packages, and holds paid promotion behind
the current service-state and live-funnel gates.

### The Launch Timeline
**EXECUTION_PLAN.md** is the operational document — who does what, which tools to use, what it costs, and how to know if it's working.

## What Gets Built First

**Priority order:**

1. **Soccer Dad script finalized** → Feed into Runway/Sora for AI video generation
2. **Short form hooks produced** → Posted immediately (TikTok + Reels + Shorts) to start building audience and pixel data
3. **Retargeting pixel setup** → Meta Pixel + TikTok Pixel configured on the funnel page
4. **Hero video goes live** (YouTube + Facebook) → Retargeted audiences from short form
5. **Funnel page live** → FUNNEL_PAGE.html deployed with Sarah AI integration
6. **Tree/Acorn spot** → Runs as brand awareness unit / pre-roll / brand page header
