---

## HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

Each scene from the Soccer Dad hero video and the Tree/Acorn brand story has been broken into individual generation prompts. For each scene you'll find:

- **Sora prompt** — Optimized for OpenAI Sora (best for longer, cinematic narrative clips)
- **Runway ML prompt** — Optimized for Runway Gen-4 (best for fast, controllable motion clips)
- **Midjourney prompt** — For key still frames, thumbnails, storyboards, and reference images

**Production tip:** Generate 3–5 variations of each scene, then select the best take. AI video is probabilistic — more generations = better final cut selection. Budget for ~30–50 clip generations for the full hero video.

**Color grading note:** Apply a warm, slightly desaturated LUT to ALL generated clips in post-production for visual consistency. Reference: "Cinematic Kodak Warm" or "Golden Hour Film" LUT packs on LUTscolor.com.

## GENERAL STYLE NOTES FOR ALL AI GENERATION

**The Evermore Life Look — apply to every prompt:**
- Warm, desaturated color palette (not oversaturated, not cold)
- Film grain texture (add if the tool supports it)
- Golden hour or soft interior light preferred
- No artificial lighting setups that look "commercial"
- Real people, not AI-looking faces (for character consistency, use the same seed in Midjourney or the same character reference in Runway)
- Shallow depth of field — backgrounds soft, subjects sharp
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 for YouTube/Facebook horizontal; 9:16 for TikTok/Reels/Shorts
- Reference films for style direction: *Boyhood*, *Tree of Life*, *Field of Dreams*, *Chef's Table* (Season 1)
