# THE TREE AND THE ACORN
## Evermore Life — Brand Identity Video Script
**Format:** Cinematic brand spot | Runtime: 60–90 seconds
**Production:** Sora/Runway for visuals + ElevenLabs for voiceover
**Version:** 1.0 — Production Draft

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Visual Style:**
Pure, slow-motion nature cinematography — the kind you'd see in a BBC nature documentary, but warmer and more intimate. No people until the final moment. The tree IS the character. Macro and wide lenses alternating. Everything in natural light — dawn light preferred. Color palette: forest green, amber, deep gold, warm brown earth.

**Audio Style:**
Single piano note to open — long sustain. Builds slowly into a minimal orchestral arrangement. No percussion until the logo reveal. The voiceover is poetic, unhurried — like someone speaking at a eulogy who has decided to be calm and true instead of dramatic.

**Pacing:**
This is the slowest content Evermore Life makes. It is not trying to sell anything immediately. It is establishing a feeling. A belief. A symbol.

**On-Screen Text:**
White serif only. One line at a time. Maximum 8 words per card. Long pauses between text appearances.

## THE SCRIPT

---

### OPENING — [0:00–0:10]

**[VISUAL]**
Black. Silence.

Then: the sound of wind through leaves — barely audible. It grows, slightly.

**[VISUAL]**
Slow fade in: a single towering oak tree at golden hour. The camera starts at the base — roots gripping earth — and slowly tilts upward. The trunk is wide. This tree is old. It has seen things. The light filters through the canopy.

**[ON-SCREEN TEXT]**
*(nothing yet — let the tree speak)*

---

### THE TREE — [0:10–0:35]

**[VISUAL]**
Macro shot: a single leaf, backlit by the sun. You can see every vein, every cell, the life in it.

**[VISUAL]**
Wide: the full tree in its environment. Other trees around it — but this one is clearly older, larger, more settled. Roots grip deep. Branches stretch wide.

**[VOICEOVER — slow, deliberate]**
"A tree doesn't grow to be remembered."

**[VISUAL]**
Time-lapse (brief, 2 seconds) — the tree through seasons. Green, gold, bare, green again.

**[VOICEOVER]**
"It grows because that's what it was made to do."

**[VISUAL]**
Rain on leaves — macro shot. Each drop bends the leaf, then releases it.

**[VOICEOVER]**
"It weathers. It endures. It stretches toward something it will never reach."

**[VISUAL]**
The canopy from below, looking up — a cathedral of branches.

**[VOICEOVER]**
"And then — when it is fully itself — it does one more thing."

---

### THE ACORN — [0:35–0:55]

**[VISUAL]**
Extreme close-up: an acorn on the branch. Still. Perfect. Small.

**[VISUAL — slow motion]**
The acorn lets go of the branch. It falls — tumbling slowly — through shafts of golden light. This shot is beautiful. Take time with it.

**[VISUAL]**
The acorn hits the earth. A tiny impact. The earth receives it.

**[ON-SCREEN TEXT — slow fade in]**
*"It drops an acorn."*

**[VISUAL]**
Time passes (brief time-lapse): soil, then a tiny shoot, impossibly green, pushing upward.

**[VOICEOVER]**
"That acorn waits. It pushes through dark and cold and resistance."

**[VISUAL]**
The sapling — young, tender, reaching for light. Then: a small tree. Then: time moves and it becomes larger, fuller.

**[VOICEOVER]**
"And it becomes a tree."

**[VISUAL]**
Wide shot: two trees. The old oak, massive and weathered. And beside it — younger, growing, reaching — a new one. The same species. The same grain. A new thing.

**[VOICEOVER]**
"Not the same tree. A new tree."

**[PAUSE — two full seconds of just the image]**

**[VOICEOVER]**
"But carrying everything that came before it."

---

### THE BRIDGE — [0:55–1:10]

**[VISUAL]**
For the first time — a human. A parent's hand, reaching down. A child's hand, reaching up. They meet. No faces yet.

**[VOICEOVER]**
"This is what love does."

**[VISUAL]**
The two hands. Held. Then the adult gently raises the child's hand — the child is lifted slightly. The motion is trust.

**[VOICEOVER]**
"It plants. It prepares. It makes sure."

**[VISUAL]**
Wide shot of a parent and child from behind, walking — silhouetted against a golden field. The parent's hand rests on the child's shoulder. They walk into the light.

**[ON-SCREEN TEXT]**
*"Your legacy moves on."*

**[HOLD — three seconds]**

---

### LOGO REVEAL — [1:10–1:25]

**[VISUAL]**
Slow fade to near-black. The Evermore Life tree logo appears — rooted, branching, alive. Growing slowly on screen.

**[ON-SCREEN TEXT — below the logo]**
*Evermore Life*

**[VOICEOVER — quieter now]**
"Evermore Life. Because love doesn't end. It grows."

**[VISUAL]**
The logo holds. Below it:

`evermorelife.com`

**[HOLD — three seconds. Fade to black.]**

## USAGE GUIDE

**Where this runs:**
- Brand awareness campaigns (cold audiences)
- Pre-roll on YouTube (first 5 seconds must hook — start with the acorn falling)
- Facebook brand page header video
- Intro to longer form content (can be attached to the front of the Soccer Dad video as a brand intro)
- Website hero video on the homepage

**What it's NOT for:**
This spot has no CTA beyond brand awareness. Do not use this for conversion campaigns. This is the impression — the emotional foundation. Let other content close.

**Versions to cut:**
1. **Full 90-second brand spot** (as scripted)
2. **30-second brand cut** (acorn falls → becomes tree → hands → logo) — for pre-roll/awareness
3. **15-second logo reveal** (the two trees → logo) — for retargeting frequency cap
