# 04 — Hero Video Script

## Working Title

**Your Legacy Moves On | Evermore Life**

## Target Length

3–5 minutes.

## Format

- 16:9 for YouTube/website.
- Cut down to 9:16 for TikTok/Reels/Shorts.
- Use cinematic AI footage, voiceover, soft music, and subtle sound design.

## Hero Video Structure

### 0:00–0:20 — Opening Symbol

**Visual:**  
Close-up of an acorn falling near the roots of a large tree. Morning light. A child’s soccer ball rolls into frame.

**Voiceover:**  
Some things do not end with us.  
They grow through the people we love.

**On-screen text:**  
Your legacy moves on.

---

### 0:20–1:10 — The Father’s Dream

**Visual:**  
A young boy playing soccer. Then teenage version scoring a goal. His father or coach cheering. Later, he becomes a young adult working long hours, putting away his cleats, choosing family responsibility.

**Voiceover:**  
He grew up with a dream.  
Every field felt like home.  
Every goal felt like proof that something inside him was alive.

But life has a way of asking us to choose.  
So he worked.  
He showed up.  
He built a life for the people who depended on him.

Not because the dream disappeared.  
Because love became bigger than the dream.

---

### 1:10–2:10 — The Son Inherits the Fire

**Visual:**  
Father sees his young son kicking a soccer ball naturally in the backyard. The son laughs. The father kneels, ties his cleats, and teaches him. Years pass through quick scenes: early morning practice, rainy field, worn-out ball, late-night homework, the father watching from the sideline after work.

**Voiceover:**  
Then one day, his son picked up the ball.

No one forced it.  
No one planned it.  
It was just there — like something passed through the blood.

So the father did what fathers do.

He drove him to practice.  
He stayed late.  
He worked extra.  
He stood behind the fence when no one else was watching.

He did not need the dream to be his anymore.  
It was enough to help it live through his son.

---

### 2:10–2:55 — The Legacy Moment

**Visual:**  
The son is now a professional soccer player. Stadium lights. He wins an award. Crowd cheering. He scans the crowd, finds his father, runs to him, and hugs him.

**Voiceover:**  
Years later, the world saw the son win.

But the son knew the truth.

Behind every trophy, there were early mornings.  
Behind every goal, there were sacrifices.  
Behind every dream, there was someone who kept showing up.

And when the moment came, the first person he looked for was his father.

**On-screen text:**  
Some dreams do not end. They move through the people we love.

---

### 2:55–3:35 — Brand Bridge

**Visual:**  
Tree growing through seasons. Parent and child walking under it. Acorn becoming a young tree.

**Voiceover:**  
That is what legacy really is.

It is not only what you achieve.  
It is what continues because you cared enough to build, protect, and prepare.

Life insurance is not about leaving.  
It is about still being there — with clarity, protection, and peace of mind for the people you love.

**On-screen text:**  
Be there evermore.

---

### 3:35–4:35 — Process Explanation

**Visual:**  
Clean Evermore Life interface. Friendly AI assistant Sarah. Simple icons: age, health, budget, goals, family, coverage options. Broker comparing policies.

**Voiceover:**  
At Evermore Life, we help you look at your options in a simple way.

We start with a few basic questions: your age, health, budget, state, and the people or expenses you want to protect.

Then we help compare options that may fit your situation.

For some people, that may mean term coverage for income or mortgage protection.  
For others, it may mean final expense coverage to help with funeral costs and final bills.  
Some may need permanent coverage, whole life, or indexed universal life depending on their goals and qualifications.

The point is not to force one product.  
The point is to help match the right type of protection to the person.

---

### 4:35–5:00 — Call to Action

**Visual:**  
Father and son photo on a mantle. Evermore tree logo. Soft fade.

**Voiceover:**  
You have spent your life showing up for the people you love.

This is one more way to show up.

Request a simple coverage review today.

**On-screen text:**  
Your legacy moves on.  
Be there evermore.  
Evermore Life

**CTA button:**  
Start My Coverage Review
