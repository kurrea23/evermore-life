# 08 — Retargeting Map

## Core Principle

Use emotional story content to build warm audiences, then retarget viewers with simple education and a clear appointment CTA.

## Audience Buckets

### Bucket 1 — Cold Audience

People who have not engaged yet.

Creative:
- Cinematic legacy stories.
- Parent/child videos.
- Evermore tree/acorn motif.

Goal:
- Video views.
- Engagement.
- Profile/page visits.

CTA:
- Soft: “Watch the story.”
- Soft: “Be there evermore.”

---

### Bucket 2 — Engaged Viewers

People who watched a meaningful portion of videos or engaged with posts.

Creative:
- “Here’s what life insurance actually does.”
- “How Evermore Life helps compare options.”
- “What affects your price?”
- “What to expect on a coverage review.”

Goal:
- Landing page visits.
- Form starts.
- Chat starts.

CTA:
- “Start a simple coverage review.”

---

### Bucket 3 — Page Visitors / Form Starts

People who visited the funnel or started but did not submit.

Creative:
- FAQ clips.
- Trust clips.
- Process clips.
- “No pressure, just clarity.”

Goal:
- Completed form.
- Appointment booking.

CTA:
- “Finish your review.”

---

### Bucket 4 — Leads Not Booked

People who submitted info but have not scheduled.

Creative:
- SMS/email reminders.
- “What happens on the call?”
- “Bring your questions.”
- “Coverage options depend on your situation.”

Goal:
- Appointment set.

CTA:
- “Pick a time.”

---

### Bucket 5 — Booked / No-Show

People who booked but missed.

Creative:
- Reschedule reminder.
- Calm reassurance.
- “Life gets busy — we can restart.”

Goal:
- Rescheduled appointment.

CTA:
- “Reschedule.”

## Retargeting Sequence

### Day 0–1

Show:
- Thank you page.
- SMS/email confirmation.
- “What happens next” clip.

### Day 2–4

Show:
- Broker process explainer.
- FAQ: “How much does it cost?”
- FAQ: “What if I have health issues?”

### Day 5–10

Show:
- Legacy story variation.
- Client-style education without testimonials unless compliant/approved.
- “Still have questions?” CTA.

### Day 11–30

Show:
- Monthly affordability framing.
- Family protection education.
- Final expense clarity video.

## Key Metrics

### Top of Funnel

- 3-second video views.
- ThruPlay / completed views.
- Average watch time.
- Engagement rate.
- Cost per engaged viewer.

### Middle of Funnel

- Landing page click-through rate.
- Cost per landing page view.
- Form start rate.
- Chat start rate.

### Bottom of Funnel

- Cost per lead.
- Contact rate.
- Appointment set rate.
- Show rate.
- Application rate.
- Issued policy rate.
- Cost per issued policy.
