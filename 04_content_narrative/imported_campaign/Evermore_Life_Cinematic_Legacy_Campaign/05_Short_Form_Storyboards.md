# 05 — Short Form Storyboards

## Short 1 — Soccer Son Legacy

**Length:** 35–45 seconds  
**Goal:** emotional top-of-funnel

### Shot List

1. Acorn falls beside tree roots.
2. Young dad as a boy scoring a soccer goal.
3. Adult dad putting cleats away before heading to work.
4. His young son kicks a ball in the backyard.
5. Dad kneels and ties son’s cleats.
6. Practice montage: morning, rain, night.
7. Adult son wins major soccer award.
8. Son runs into crowd and hugs dad.
9. Tree/acorn logo ending.

### Voiceover

He once had a dream of his own.  
Then life gave him something bigger to protect.

Years later, his son picked up the same dream — not because he was pushed, but because something inside him already knew.

So his father showed up.  
Practice after practice.  
Shift after shift.  
Year after year.

When the world finally cheered for his son, the first person he ran to was his dad.

Some dreams do not end.  
They move through the people we love.

**On-screen text:**  
Your legacy moves on.  
Be there evermore.
