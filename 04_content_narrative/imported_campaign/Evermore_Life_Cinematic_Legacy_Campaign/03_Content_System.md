# 03 — Content System

## Campaign Architecture

This campaign has three content layers:

### Layer 1 — Cinematic Emotional Storytelling

Purpose: stop the scroll and create emotional investment.

Formats:
- 15–45 second TikToks/Reels/Shorts.
- 60–90 second extended short.
- 3–5 minute hero video.

Primary emotion:
- Love.
- Gratitude.
- Family.
- Legacy.
- Protection.

### Layer 2 — Simple Life Insurance Education

Purpose: turn emotional attention into informed trust.

Formats:
- 30–90 second talking-head or AI-presented videos.
- Carousel posts.
- Website FAQs.
- SMS/email education sequence.

Topics:
- “What does life insurance actually do?”
- “How much coverage do I need?”
- “What affects your monthly price?”
- “Term vs final expense vs whole life vs IUL.”
- “What happens on the first call?”
- “Why comparing carriers matters.”

### Layer 3 — Direct Response / Appointment Setting

Purpose: convert warm viewers into leads and appointments.

Formats:
- Retargeting ads.
- Funnel page.
- AI chat assistant.
- Form.
- SMS/email follow-up.
- Appointment booking.

CTA examples:
- “See what coverage may fit your family.”
- “Request a simple coverage review.”
- “Compare options with a licensed broker.”
- “Start with a few basic questions.”

## Weekly Posting Rhythm

### Daily

- 1 emotional short or family legacy clip.
- 1 educational micro-clip or FAQ answer.
- 1 story/caption post.

### Weekly

- 1 longer 2–5 minute video.
- 1 full educational breakdown.
- 1 behind-the-brand trust post.

## Organic Caption Formula

```text
Hook: A line that hits emotionally.
Story: 2–4 lines expanding the moment.
Bridge: Connect the story to protection/legacy.
CTA: Soft invitation.
```

### Example

> He gave up his dream so his son could chase his.  
> Years later, the first person his son looked for was him.  
> That is legacy.  
> Life insurance is one more way to keep showing up.  
> Be there evermore.

## Paid Retargeting Creative Formula

```text
You watched the story.
Now here is the practical side.
Coverage may be simpler than you think.
Answer a few questions and we can help compare options.
```

## Production Rule

Every cinematic short should be built from the hero video world.

Do not make disconnected one-off clips.  
Build a repeatable Evermore Life “universe” with consistent symbols:

- Warm morning light.
- Family home.
- Tree/acorn.
- Parent/child hands.
- Old photos.
- Childhood dream object.
- Final hug.
- Evermore Life closing card.
