# 11 — Compliance Checklist

This is an operational checklist, not legal advice.

## Insurance Advertising Rules to Respect

- [ ] Do not promise approval unless the exact product allows it and limitations are disclosed.
- [ ] Do not say “everyone qualifies.”
- [ ] Do not use exact premium examples without clear context.
- [ ] Do not imply a policy is free.
- [ ] Do not hide that the offer is insurance.
- [ ] Do not use fake testimonials.
- [ ] Do not use fabricated client stories as if they are real.
- [ ] Do not imply Evermore Life is a carrier if it is an agency/brokerage.
- [ ] Do not mention carriers unless you are contracted and allowed to advertise them.
- [ ] Do not request sensitive financial or medical information directly inside ad copy.
- [ ] Use proper SMS consent language before texting marketing messages.
- [ ] Make sure Privacy Policy and Terms pages are live and linked.
- [ ] Keep all claims clear, truthful, and substantiated.

## Platform Ad Safety

### Meta

- [ ] Use the correct Special Ad Category for financial/insurance campaigns when required.
- [ ] Target insurance ads only to adults.
- [ ] Avoid direct requests for personally identifiable information inside the ad itself.
- [ ] Keep landing page consistent with the ad.
- [ ] Avoid misleading “too good to be true” hooks.
- [ ] Create video engagement audiences from compliant videos when available.

### TikTok

- [ ] Follow financial services advertising requirements.
- [ ] Be transparent that the offer relates to insurance.
- [ ] Avoid misleading claims or pressure tactics.
- [ ] Confirm whether approval/authorization is required for financial services ads in your target market.

## Recommended Funnel Disclaimer

> Coverage availability, policy type, benefits, premiums, and approval depend on age, health, state, carrier underwriting, product type, and other eligibility factors. Not all applicants qualify. Evermore Life may work with multiple carriers to help compare available options. This is a solicitation for insurance.

## Recommended AI Chat Disclaimer

> I can help collect basic information and explain the process, but final recommendations, pricing, eligibility, and applications should be reviewed with a licensed insurance professional.

## Recommended Caption Disclaimer

> Coverage options vary by age, health, state, product type, carrier, and eligibility. Not all applicants qualify.

## Safer Emotional Framing

Use:
- “protect”
- “prepare”
- “clarity”
- “peace of mind”
- “showing up”
- “legacy”
- “family protection”

Avoid overusing:
- “burden”
- “death”
- “leave them with nothing”
- “your family will suffer”
- “before it’s too late”
