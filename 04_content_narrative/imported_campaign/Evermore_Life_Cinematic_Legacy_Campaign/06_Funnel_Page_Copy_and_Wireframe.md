## Section 4 — Coverage Types We May Review

### Final Expense

Designed to help loved ones with funeral costs, burial/cremation costs, and other final bills.

### Term Life

Often used for mortgage protection, income replacement, family protection, or coverage during key earning years.

### Whole Life / Permanent Coverage

Coverage that may last for life when premiums are paid, depending on the policy.

### Indexed Universal Life / Other Options

For qualified clients with certain long-term goals, we may review additional options when appropriate.

## Section 7 — FAQ

### How much does life insurance cost?

It depends on your age, health, state, coverage amount, product type, and carrier. The purpose of the review is to help compare what may be available to you.

### Do I have to choose a policy today?

No. The first step is understanding your options.

### What if I have health issues?

Some products are more flexible than others. We review your situation and help identify options you may qualify for.

### Is Evermore Life an insurance company?

Evermore Life is an insurance brokerage/agency brand that helps people review coverage options. Carrier availability may vary.
