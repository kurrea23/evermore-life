# Evermore Life — Cinematic Legacy Campaign Folder

**Created:** 2026-05-03  
**Core campaign idea:** cinematic family legacy stories that make life insurance feel like an act of love, responsibility, and continuation — not a cold financial product.

## Folder Purpose

This folder turns the raw voice-note idea into an executable content and funnel system:

1. Build emotional narrative assets.
2. Produce AI-generated cinematic footage.
3. Post short and long-form content organically.
4. Retarget high-engagement viewers.
5. Send warm traffic to the Evermore Life funnel.
6. Use AI/chat + forms to gather basic info and set appointments.
7. Educate prospects on product fit: final expense, term, whole life, IUL, or other suitable coverage.

## First Execution Goal

Produce one complete campaign unit:

- 1 hero video, 3–5 minutes.
- 7 short story clips, 15–45 seconds each.
- 5 educational clips, 30–90 seconds each.
- 1 funnel page.
- 1 AI/chat appointment flow.
- 1 retargeting sequence for viewers who watched most of the emotional story content.

## Important Operating Rule

Do **not** lead with fear. Lead with love, legacy, and responsibility.  
The emotional hook should create reflection, not panic.
