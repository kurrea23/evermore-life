# 10 — AI Footage Prompt Pack

## Visual Style Bible

Use this style across all assets:

> Cinematic realistic family legacy commercial, warm natural light, emotionally grounded, premium insurance brand feel, shallow depth of field, soft film grain, hopeful tone, no exaggerated drama, authentic human emotion, subtle golden-hour lighting, clean composition, modern American family home, symbolic oak tree/acorn motif, commercial-quality color grading.

## Negative Prompt / Avoid

Avoid:
- Distorted faces.
- Extra fingers.
- Fake-looking smiles.
- Overdramatic crying.
- Text artifacts.
- Logos on clothing.
- Unrealistic stadium branding.
- Horror lighting.
- Manipulative fear-based visuals.
- Hospital/deathbed imagery unless legally reviewed and used carefully.

## Character Bible — Father

A warm, hardworking father in his late 30s to early 50s as the story progresses. Athletic background, humble, emotionally present, slightly tired from work but loving and steady. Wardrobe: simple work shirt, casual jeans, soccer jacket, neutral colors. Face: kind, grounded, proud.

## Character Bible — Son

Starts as a playful 6-year-old boy, then becomes a focused teenager, then a professional soccer player in his 20s. He has natural joy for soccer, strong relationship with father, humble and grateful. Wardrobe: youth soccer clothes, practice gear, then generic professional-style uniform with no real team logos.

## Editing Notes

- Use generated clips as visual plates.
- Keep each clip 3–8 seconds.
- Avoid long AI shots where faces break.
- Use slow push-ins and crossfades.
- Use hands, objects, and tree symbolism to reduce AI face consistency problems.
- Add real voiceover and music after the edit.
- Build 9:16 vertical cuts from the strongest emotional moments.
