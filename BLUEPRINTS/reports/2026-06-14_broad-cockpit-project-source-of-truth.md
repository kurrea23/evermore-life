much older May 11 cockpit page, so stale EDC, IRS, A2P, and contracting content
kept surviving under the new date stamp. The local cockpit now renders from the
current broad snapshot and exposes `Main`, `Projects`, and `Done` so completed
items can drop out of active view instead of remaining as fake blockers.
