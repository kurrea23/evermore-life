# A2P Messaging Use Case Paste Copy

## Campaign Use Case

Customer Care

## Use Case Description

Evermore Life LLC sends SMS messages only to people who submit a quote request and voluntarily opt in on our website at https://evermorelife.org/home or https://evermorelife.org/optin. Messages are used to confirm receipt of the request, coordinate appointment times, answer questions, and follow up about life insurance coverage options related to the person's inquiry. SMS consent is optional and is not required to request a quote. Messages include opt-out instructions such as STOP and help instructions such as HELP.

## Sample Message #1

Hi {{contact.first_name}}, this is Evermore Life LLC. We received your quote request and a licensed agent will follow up shortly. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.

## Sample Message #2

Hi {{contact.first_name}}, this is Evermore Life LLC following up on your quote request. Please reply here if you have questions or need to reschedule. Reply STOP to opt out.

## Additional Message Details

- Embedded link: Check this if messages will include links such as evermorelife.org or booking links.
- Phone number: Check this only if messages will include a phone number in the message body.
- Age gated content: Do not check.
- Financial services / loan arrangement: Do not check unless the platform specifically tells you insurance/annuity follow-up must be classified there. Do not send loan, debt, credit, payday loan, mortgage loan, crypto, or investment-promotion texts under this campaign.

## Website Form SMS Checkbox Copy

Optional: I consent to receive SMS messages from Evermore Life LLC about my quote request, appointment reminders, and related coverage follow-up at the phone number I provide. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help or STOP to opt out. Consent is not a condition of purchase. See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Checklist:

- Checkbox must be optional.
- Checkbox must be unchecked by default.
- Do not make SMS consent required to submit the form.
