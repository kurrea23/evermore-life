We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To send informational and promotional communications about our products and services when permitted.
  • To comply with applicable legal, regulatory, and licensing requirements.

4. SMS / Text Message Communications

If you voluntarily check an SMS consent box on one of our forms, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages from Evermore Life, including responses to your quote request, follow-up from licensed agents, appointment reminders, and information about coverage options related to your inquiry.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier plan.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any text message at any time. After opting out, you may receive one final confirmation message and will receive no further texts from us unless you opt in again.

For help: Reply HELP to any message or contact us at evermorelifeagent01@gmail.com.

We do not share, sell, rent, or use your mobile phone number for any purpose other than the communications described above. Your opt-in to SMS is not a condition of purchasing any insurance product or service.

SMS consent data: All categories above exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent. SMS opt-in data and consent will not be shared with third parties or marketers for their own marketing or promotional purposes.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:
