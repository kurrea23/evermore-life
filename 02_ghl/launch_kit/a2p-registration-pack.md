## Campaign Description

`Evermore Life sends SMS messages to individuals who voluntarily request a life insurance quote, appointment, or follow-up through our website form. Messages include requested quote follow-up, appointment reminders, and help completing the insurance inquiry process. Users can request a quote without consenting to SMS, and SMS consent is collected through an optional, unchecked checkbox on the web form.`

## Opt-In Flow

`A visitor goes to https://evermorelife.org/optin, enters their contact information in the Evermore Life Quote Request form, and may optionally check an unchecked SMS consent checkbox. The checkbox states: "Optional: I consent to receive SMS text messages from Evermore Life about my quote request, appointment reminders, and insurance information. Msg & data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase." The form includes visible Privacy Policy and Terms of Service links. The user can submit the quote request without checking the SMS consent box.`

## Opt-In Confirmation Message

`Evermore Life: Thanks for requesting a life insurance quote. Sarah here. I can help you review options and book a quick call. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help. Msg/data rates may apply.`
